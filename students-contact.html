Information for Students

I welcome email from students both at USC and elsewhere. I'm especially interested in working with students who have a curiosity about the world around them and a drive to build things.

  • USC undergraduates. Whether you'd like to chat with me about a crazy idea you have or would like to join my research group, feel free to send me an email. I also encourage you to take one of my classes as that will let you get to know my interests and style of work.
  • USC masters students. I am open to working with masters students who have demonstrated very strong engineering skills; please feel free to send me an email.
  • Prospective PhD students. I am always looking for students to join our research group; I would recommend gaining familiarity with my research and interests before sending me an email.
